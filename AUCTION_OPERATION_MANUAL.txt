# THE AUC - OFFICIAL OPERATION MANUAL

This manual provides a step-by-step guide to conducting your auction smoothly. You will be using two separate laptops:
1. HOST LAPTOP: Used by the auctioneer to control everything (bids, picking players, assigning teams).
2. LIVE PROJECTOR LAPTOP: Connected to a projector/big screen for the audience to watch the event unfold.

=======================================================
PART 1: PRE-AUCTION PREPARATION & SETUP
=======================================================

1. LINK SETUP
   - Both laptops need internet access.
   - Live Projector Laptop: Open the website and navigate to the Home page. Keep it in Full Screen mode (F11 on Windows).
   - Host Laptop: Open the website and navigate to the "Host Panel" or "Setup" page. 

2. PRE-SHOW: HALL OF FAME & GALLERY
   - Before the auction begins, use the Live Projector Laptop to show the audience the Hall of Fame and Gallery.
   - Navigate to the "Hall of Fame" page to showcase past winners and legends.
   - Navigate to the "Gallery" page to show memories and hype up the audience.
   - Once you are ready to begin the actual event, navigate the Live Projector Laptop to the "Live" page.

3. TOURNAMENT SETUP (HOST LAPTOP)
   - On the Host Laptop, go to the Setup page.
   - Ensure your tournament data is correctly loaded (Teams, Players, Budget, Slots).
   - Enter your "Tournament Rules" in the Setup page. This will be displayed to the audience later.

=======================================================
PART 2: STARTING THE AUCTION
=======================================================

1. THE WAITING SCREEN
   - On the Live Projector Laptop, the "Live" screen will show a "Waiting for Host" message and will automatically display the Tournament Rules for the audience to read while they settle down.

2. INITIATING THE AUCTION
   - On the Host Laptop, navigate to the "Host Panel".
   - You will see a "START AUCTION" button. 
   - When the crowd is ready, click "START AUCTION".
   - **What happens next:** The Live screen will instantly trigger a dramatic 3-second countdown. Once the countdown finishes, the first random player will be revealed on the big screen with a golden "Matrix-style" scramble animation!

=======================================================
PART 3: CONDUCTING BIDS (THE MAIN EVENT)
=======================================================

1. THE PLAYER REVEAL
   - The system automatically picks a random player. Neither you nor the audience knows who is coming up next until the scramble animation locks in.
   - The Host Laptop will show the player's details, base price, and stats.

2. TAKING BIDS
   - The current bid always starts at the player's Base Price.
   - Below the current bid, you will see the active "Increment Rules" (e.g., +100 if bid is below 1000, +200 if above 1000).
   - When a team raises their paddle to bid, **click on that Team's Card** on the Host Laptop.
   - The bid will automatically increase by the exact increment amount. The Live screen will instantly update with an animation showing which team took the lead.
   - *Tip:* The Host screen shows which teams cannot afford the next bid, or whose gender slots are already full. Their cards will be faded out and unclickable to prevent mistakes.

3. SOLD OR UNSOLD
   - Once the bidding stops, you have two options:
     - **SOLD:** Click the green "SOLD" button. Confetti will explode on the Live screen, and the player is assigned to the winning team.
     - **UNSOLD:** If no one bids the base price, click the orange "UNSOLD" button. The player will be sent to the "Chit Round" for later.

4. THE TIMER
   - You have a timer on the Host screen (Play/Pause/Reset). Use this to build suspense ("Going once... going twice..."). It automatically resets to 30 seconds for the next player.

5. NEXT PLAYER
   - Once a player is Sold or Unsold, click the "Next Player" button to bring up the next random player with another scramble animation.

=======================================================
PART 4: THE CHIT ROUND (OVERRIDE MODE)
=======================================================
If any players went "Unsold" during the main auction, you will be directed to the Chit Round to assign them to teams that are still missing players.

1. HOW IT WORKS
   - The system automatically calculates which teams still need players to fill their required slots.
   - It places these incomplete teams into a "Randomized Turn Queue".
   - The UI will explicitly tell you whose turn it is: **"Current Turn: [Team Name]"**

2. PICKING A CHIT
   - The Host clicks on any of the hidden mystery chits.
   - The player is revealed and is **automatically forced into the active team**. 
   - Even if the team already has the maximum number of players for that gender, the system will force the override to ensure everyone gets a team, keeping the total player count mathematically balanced.
   - The turn will automatically pass to the next team in the queue. 
   - The queue loops continuously until all chits are opened or all teams are full.

=======================================================
PART 5: ASSIGNING TEAM COLORS & THE GRAND FINALE
=======================================================
Once all players are assigned, you proceed to the "Colors" page to assign T-shirt colors to the teams.

1. COLOR ASSIGNMENT
   - On the Host Laptop, pick a color for a team.
   - When you click "Reveal Color", the Live screen will show a dramatic spinning wheel animation that lands on the chosen color, followed by a cinematic reveal of the team's entire roster.
   - Repeat this for all teams.

2. EDIT MODE (EMERGENCIES ONLY)
   - If you ever make a mistake (e.g., assigning a player to the wrong team, or needing to fix a budget), click the small gear/settings icon on the Host Panel.
   - Enter your password to access "Edit Mode" where you can manually fix player statuses, sold prices, team budgets, and bid increments on the fly without breaking the auction flow.

=======================================================
PRO-TIPS FOR A SMOOTH EVENT
=======================================================
- **Keep both laptops plugged in** so they don't go to sleep.
- **Do not refresh the Host page unnecessarily**, though if you do, your state is safely saved in the database and will restore instantly.
- **Communicate with the projector operator:** If you need them to scroll or show something specific on the Live screen, give them a subtle signal. (Though the Live screen is 99% automated!)
- If the Live screen seems out of sync (which is rare), just press `F5` on the Live Projector Laptop to refresh. It will instantly snap back to the exact current state of the auction.
